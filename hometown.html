My hometown,Kampung Sungai Mati is located in Mata Ayer, Perlis. It is a small village surrounded by paddy field. It also has a very beautiful view of the hills. My villagers are very friendly and helpful. They are willing to offer mutual help in any case and always get on with one another harmoniously. My village is rather small indeed but I like it very much because I grown up here and spent my happiest childhood among the simple and hard-working villagers who always feel attached to their native land.
